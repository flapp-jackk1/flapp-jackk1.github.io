Research

Nova-Labs • Space is an independent student-driven astronomy and space research initiative focused on exoplanet detection and analysis, as well as deepening understanding of our universe.

Our current focus includes:

  • Transit Method for Exoplanet Detection
  • Mathematical modelling and data analysis techniques
  • Developing software tools for astronomy research

Projects

We are currently developing:

  • Open-source Python tools for transit light curve analysis
  • Simulation models for spacecraft trajectory optimization
  • Collaborative databases for astronomical observations
