About Nova-Labs Space

Nova-Labs Space, or NLS for short, is a small independent research project, focusing on the fields of astronomy and astrophysics. Founded 28 May, 2025. Started off as a small project on the backburner, however small we may be, we have big goals, which can only be supported by YOU.
