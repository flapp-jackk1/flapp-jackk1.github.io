About Nova-Labs • Space

NovaLabs • Space is a student led initiative, created to spread the word about how exciting the field of astronomy and space can truly be. This project started off as nothing, just a small idea on the backburner. Until it wasn't. NovaLabs has evolved into something great and unique, with aims to explore space and astronomy using the latest scientific techniques and tools.
